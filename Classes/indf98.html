I am currently a tenure-track assistant professor at Cal State, Hayward, where I arrived in 1997. Before arriving here, I almost circled the globe in an easterly fashion. After growing up in Chicago, I went to school at Swarthmore College and MIT . I received my Ph.D. in algebraic combinatorics under Richard Stanley in 1991, then continued eastward to positions at the University of Tokyo and Reed College , eventually arriving 160 miles northwest of where I started, as a research associate at the University of Wisconsin (1995-97).
