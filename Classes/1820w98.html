Class Information

HOMEWORK ASSIGNMENTS:
Homework will be given for each lecture, and all the homework assigned the previous week will be due the following Wednesday. Except for routine computations, you should always give reasons to support your work and explain what you're doing. Not all the problems will be graded, but only a small subset; most of the ones assigned have answers in the back so you can tell if you're on the right track. This makes it more important that you write your solutions carefully. Late homework cannot be accepted, but your lowest two homework assignments will be dropped.
